Privacy Policy

This app is used by the app owner to publish posts to a Threads account through the Threads API.

The app requests access only for authentication and publishing content authorized by the account owner.

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for advertising. Access tokens are stored securely by the app owner and can be revoked at any time from Meta account settings.

For privacy or deletion requests, contact the app owner at the email listed in the Meta app settings.
